Affirmative.

Affirmative is a positive thinking app for IOS that emulates a deck of affirmations. In other words, it’s a collection of positive statements presented in a playing card format that the user can then read, collect, and share. More importantly, Affirmative houses a growing library of resources committed to assisting those strugglings with a wide variety of mental health issues.


My role: User research, user flows, information architecture, sketching, wireframing, brand development, UI design, prototyping, usability testing.

The Challenge

A handful of applications exist that present affirmations, but there are few that give their users the option to reach out for further assistance when their self-help app doesn’t solve all of their problems. The primary challenge would be to design a system that strikes a balance between promoting the benefits of positive thinking and acknowledging the pitfalls of relying upon it for serious issues.

The Goal

1. Design an iOS app in the mental wellness genre that breaks the mold of self-help cliches.

2. Create a game-like experience that attracts a wide user pool, but doesn’t alienate more serious users.

3. Maintain a continuously growing library that provides specified mental wellness resources, accounting for people from a wide swath of races, gender identities, sexual orientations, religious affiliations, and age groups.

User surveys

The potential users I surveyed yielded results that helped expound on these functions, and provided insight into how the presentation of said functions could effect the experience.


Notable results and quotes:

User Stories

I need to be able to...

Access mental health resources

So that I have the ability to seek help from professionals equipped to properly assess my particular issue.

- Ensure that the library of resources is front-facing, easy to access, and easy to navigate.

I need to be able to...

Quickly be shown a positive affirmation

So that I can feel calmer, happier, or better about myself.

- Allow users to bypass onboarding, or make the sign in/sign up process as brief as possible.

I want to be able to...

Create my own custome affirmation

So that I record and recall a positive statement that is particularly effective for me.

- Allow users to personalize their own affirmation messages by providing an easy to access "add affirmation" feature.
